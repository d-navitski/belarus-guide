The Stalin Line (35km from Minsk)

The Stalin Line is the chain of defensive installations along the former USSR frontier from Karelian Isthmus to the shores of the Black Sea. Four fortified regions were situated on the territory of Belarus including Minsk fortified region. Here you will see pillboxes, semicaponiers, entrenchments, anti-tank ditches, war-time cars and aircrafts.

The historical and cultural complex was founded for the 60th anniversary of the Victory of Soviet people in the Great Patriotic War. It was open in memory of Minsk defenders on the 30th of June 2005.
