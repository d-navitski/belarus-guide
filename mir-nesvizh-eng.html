UNESCO World Cultural Heritage

Mir Castle (90 km from Minsk)

The gothic castle of the 16th century owned by prominent lineages. You will have the opportunity to explore its dungeons and visit the exhibitions of ancient weapons and armor, climb the narrow spiral staircase to the tower. Here you will hear the history of the Radziwills, the uncrowned kings as they were called.

The burial vault of the Svyatopolk-Mirsky family, last owners of the castle, is worth visiting as well. You will be impressed by the mosaic of Christ Pantocrator (Almighty). Wherever you go His eyes will necessarily meet yours.

Here you will learn the legends that local places keep,the mystery of the Castle pond and you will see the stone that is supposed to protect the whole castle!

Nesvizh Castle (120км form Minsk, 30км from Mir Castle)

At first you will get acquainted with the town of Nesvizh where Nesvizh Castle is situated. The construction of this residence started in 1583 and from this time on there happened mysterious events or heroes conducted their feats. You will see how people lived and entertained themselves, what they ate and were fond of several centuries ago. You will visit Corpus Christi Church with the burial vault of the Radziwills which is unique for our country! You will have a stroll around amazing parks of the castle and learn about the people who contributed greatly to the development of the castle, town and the country!

There is a special atmosphere and you will definitely feel it!!!
