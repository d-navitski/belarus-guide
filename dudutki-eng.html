Travel to Dudutki (40km from Minsk)

Museum complex of ancient crafts and technologies

Here you will have the opportunity to get acquainted with the occupations of our ancestors! In the wood-making workshop you will see our traditional instruments as well as different wooden appliances used in the household. The potter and the blacksmith will show their art. You will hear the legends about the dark forces living in the windmill but you will be told how to protect yourselves and even how to make your dreams come true there.

You can try the national drink, bread, cheese and butter produced on the territory of the museum! There is also a unique church. The description is just the beginning!

Discover more and become closer to your dreams!
