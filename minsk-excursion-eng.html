Minsk - the capital of the Republic of Belarus

Duration: 3 hours

During the excursion we will drive along Independence and Winners` avenues which are the two main streets of the city with the most important objects situated there. We will take a stroll around the Upper Town that was the cultural, economic and social centre in the 16th century and see the renovated Town Hall, Guest House, Church of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Holy Spirit Cathedral and former monastery complexes. We`ll get acquainted with the modern centre (Independence Square) as well with Church of Saints Simon and Helen, Government House.

We will feel the atmosphere of the 19th century in Trinity Suburb and honour the memory of heroes on the Island of Tears.

We will see modern Minsk with its sport venues and the pride of our country, the diamond of the National Library of the Republic of Belarus.

During the excursion you will be able to create an impression of historical Minsk, of Minsk of the 21st century, of the Hero City and just an amazing capital of the Republic of Belarus!
